Overview

The Bermuda Triangle, also known as the Devil's Triangle is a region in the North Atlantic Ocean,bounded by Florida, Bermuda, and Puerto Rico.

Incidents

USS Cyclops (1918)

A massive cargo ship carrying over 300 crew members disappeared without a trace while en route from Barbados to Baltimore.

Flight 19 (1945)

A group of five U.S. Navy bombers vanished during a training mission. The rescue plane sent to find them also disappeared.

Theories

Magnetic Anomalies

Some theories suggest that magnetic disturbances affect navigation instruments, causing disorientation.

Natural Causes

Sudden storms, rogue waves, and underwater earthquakes may account for many of the disappearances.
